COMP 2156 is a DevOps course that introduces students to modern
software development and system administration practices.

The course focuses on version control using Git, collaboration
through GitHub, and automation using CI/CD tools.
Students learn how DevOps improves teamwork, efficiency,
and reliability in software development projects. The course helps students understand real-world DevOps workflows used in the industry.


Students gain hands-on experience with CI/CD pipelines and automation tools. COMP 2156 prepares students for DevOps roles by emphasizing collaboration and automation.
